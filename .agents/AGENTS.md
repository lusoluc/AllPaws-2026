# Project Rules: Herzensprojekt-DNA

This project is a labor of love developed by passionate volunteers and cat lovers to aid animal shelters (such as "Būk mano draugas" in Lithuania). All copywriting, UI/UX design, and codebase edits must adhere to these rules.

## 1. Tonalität & Sprache (Menschlich & Authentisch)
- **Purge Sterile Tech Jargon**: Do not use corporate, SaaS, or AI-like phrasing (e.g., avoid "Unsere Mission ist es...", "Effiziente Optimierung von Prozessen", "Skalierbare Plattformlösungen").
- **Wohnzimmer-Stil (Living-Room Style)**: Write honest, warm, approachable, and friendly copy. Speak as if talking to a close friend.
- **Emotional Honesty**: Be transparent and open about the real effort behind the project—the team goes to its physical and financial limits to protect these animals.
- **Emotional CTAs (Call to Actions)**:
  - Instead of generic/corporate links (e.g., "Jetzt registrieren", "Spenden"), use highly emotional ones (e.g., "Werd Teil der Rettungscrew", "Unterstütze unsere Herzensarbeit").

## 2. Struktur & Inhalt (Fokus auf das "Warum" und die Tiere)
- **Hero-Section Focus**: Always highlight the volunteer nature and the passionate team behind the app right away. Explain the *Why* immediately.
- **Micro-Storytelling**: Integrate spots for real cat stories, before-and-after rescues, and direct field updates from the partner shelters.
- **High Transparency**: Make it clear that every single cent, hour, and line of code is privately contributed and 100% goes directly to the animals.

## 3. Design & UI
- **Warm & Organic Aesthetics**: Move away from cold, clinical SaaS dashboard layouts. Use soft, warm, friendly elements (amber, emerald green, warm stone, soft borders).
- **Authentic Photography**: Prefer imperfect, real-life smartphone snapshots from the shelters over polished, artificial stock photos.
